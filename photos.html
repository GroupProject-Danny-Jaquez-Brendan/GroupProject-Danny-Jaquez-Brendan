Highpoint Tournament Champions

The UNC Club Baseball team placed 1st overall in their first tournament of the fall season. ALl around, everyone played great. The team finished with a record of 3-1. The first game UNC fell to Highpoint, but went on to beat Maryland and Radford to secure their spot in the championship. In the championship game, UNC played the University of South Carolina and won with a score of 13-3.
